3. Project Summary:
The project aims to integrate ServiceNow with AppviewX to streamline SSL certificate management by automating certificate request, renewal, and ticket creation processes to address and manage certificate expiration issues efficiently. This integration will facilitate automatic ticket generation for devices with expiring certificates and streamline the workflow for certificate management within the organization.
